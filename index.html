1
Select & Pass

Click a player with the ball, then click a teammate to pass.

2

Blue attacks the BOTTOM goal. Red attacks the TOP goal. Click inside the goal area to shoot.

3
Interception

If your pass crosses an opponent, they intercept the ball!

4
Goal!

Score in the opponent's goal. The goalkeeper restarts play.
